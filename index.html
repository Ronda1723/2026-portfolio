04 — Things I believe
  1. i. Latency is a feature. Most of the magic happens under 300ms.
  2. ii. Voice is not a chatbot with a mic. It's a different posture toward software.
  3. iii. Ship the Mac and the Windows build together. Half-shipped is unshipped.
  4. iv. Tests on every change. Plan before code. Ask after the second stuck attempt.
  5. v. Memory beats prompts. The interesting AI products are the ones that remember.
