05 Writing

In notes & in margins.

A few pieces — from latency obsessions to thoughts on what makes voice products feel alive instead of useful.

  • i.Latency is a feature. Most of the magic happens under 300ms.
  • ii.Voice is not a chatbot with a mic — it's a different posture toward software.
  • iii.Ship the Mac and the Windows build together. Half‑shipped is unshipped.
  • iv.Memory beats prompts. The interesting AI products are the ones that remember.
  • v.Plan before code. Tests on every change. Ask after the second stuck attempt.
